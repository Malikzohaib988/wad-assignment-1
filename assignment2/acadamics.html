Academics

The department offers PhD, MPhil, MS (IST), M.Sc. and BS degrees.
Each degree programme has a specific objective and focus.
The course of study and syllabi for each degree is updated and is in line with its objectives.

PhD is the highest degree awarded in an academic discipline. Students work closely with faculty, performing original research and solving complex problems.

(More details..)

MPhil aims to train students in research. It spans 2 years (4 semesters), with 50 credit hours divided between coursework and research.

(More details..)

The MS (IST) program integrates IT and human-centered computing to design advanced information systems. Suitable for those with prior computing degrees and experience in digital information management.

(More details..)

The MS (Data Science) program equips students with knowledge of data analysis, visualization, and big data techniques to prepare them for industry or research careers.

(More details..)

The BS Computer Science program offers a balance of theory and practice to prepare students for industry or further studies. Focus areas include:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Software Engineering
  • Data Science
  • Human Centered Computing
  • Platform based Development
  • Networks and Security
  • Information Management
